    \| → \\| #

    \[ C(w,b) = \frac{1}{2n} \sum_x{{\|y(x)-a\|}^2} \]

    \\ at EOL → \\\\\\ #

    \begin{align} a^1 &= x \\\
    a^2 &= σ(W^2a^1 + b^2) \\\
    a^3 &= σ(W^3a^2 + b^3) \\\
    ⋯ \\\
    a^L &= σ(W^La^{L-1} + b^L) \\\
    y &= a^L \end{align}

    Same as above, but without space before the \\ at EOL #

    a^3 &= σ(W^3a^2 + b^3)\\\
    ⋯\\\
    a^L &= σ(W^La^{L-1} + b^L)\\\
